Who Are we?

Transport S&H is a privately owned Freight Forwarding firm based out of Canada, specializing in shipping solutions for Small/Medium/Large businesses. Transport S&H prides itself on its unparalleled service level, work ethic, and the most competitive pricing around the World. Our goal is to continue to disrupt the market with our innovations which in turn gives us the ability to better serve our client(s).

Our ever-growing list of official agents gives us the power to virtually ship from anywhere in the World to anywhere in the World. We are the premier go-to forwarder for time sensitive shipments in industries spanning from the Military, to Professional sports teams. With direct relationships with the heads of airlines, Transport S&H has the clear edge on the airfreight, courier, trucking and ocean freight markets.

In such an old and slow advancing industry, Transport S&H’s young a vibrant team is ready to take on any challenge(s) that’s brought upon us.
